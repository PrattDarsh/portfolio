Case Study: Crafting an Engaging Portfolio for Babu Vittal

Project Overview

Babu Vittal, a senior HR leader, needed a personal portfolio website to showcase his expertise, thought leadership, and achievements in the HR domain. The goal was to create an elegant, user-friendly site that highlighted his accomplishments and skills in a way that would appeal to both professional networks and industry audiences. I designed and developed a fully responsive website that conveys his personal brand and expertise effectively.

Babu's Website

Objectives

Establish a Professional Online Presence

Develop a site that accurately reflects Babu’s professional image.

Showcase Achievements and Expertise

Highlight key achievements, publications, and thought leadership in the HR industry.

Enhance User Experience:

Prioritize clean navigation and easy access to essential content for visitors.

User personas

Human Resources

Priya Sharma

Age: 38

Role: HR Leader

Pain Points: Difficulty finding a centralized source for insights from industry experts.

Goal: Discover thought leadership content and explore potential collaborations with Babu.

Recruitment

Rahul Verma

Age: 42

Role: Recruitment Manager

Pain Points: Challenges in assessing professional credibility quickly through scattered information.

Goal: Evaluate Babu’s experience and industry impact for potential leadership or advisory roles.

HR Education

Ananya Gupta

Age: 24

Role: HR Student

Pain Points: Overwhelming information from multiple sources with little practical application guidance.

Goal: Learn from Babu’s career journey and gain actionable knowledge for professional growth.

Event Management

Sanjay Mehta

Age: 45

Role: Conference Organizer

Pain Points:Struggles to verify speaker credentials and assess relevance to conference topics.

Goal: Find a credible and engaging speaker to add value to industry events..

Research & Discovery

Reviewed competitor portfolios and influential HR leaders' websites to identify the best features for a professional portfolio in the HR industry.

Analyzed user behavior to determine content structure and key elements for engagement, such as easy-to-navigate sections, mobile responsiveness, and visually impactful design.

Design & Development Process

Information Architecture

• Organized the site into well-defined sections such as Biography, Publications, Experience, and Speaking Engagements to allow users to find relevant information quickly.

Visual Design

• Opted for a clean, modern aesthetic using a minimalistic color palette that reflects professionalism and readability.

Responsive Design

• Developed a fully responsive layout to ensure smooth browsing on both mobile and desktop devices.

SEO Optimization

• Implemented best practices in SEO, ensuring the site ranks well on search engines to increase visibility.

Conclusion
